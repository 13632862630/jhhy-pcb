ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Shenzhen JHHY Circuit Technology Co., Ltd. devotes to technology innovation and to be an outstanding PCB manufacturer & service supplier. Established in 2005 and headquartered in Shenzhen, JHHY has grown into a high-tech enterprise and owns two factories which are located in Dongguan and Shenzhen respectively. Our PCB are widely applied in communication, industrial control, power electronics, medical instrument, security electronics, aerospace and so on. 80% of them are exported to America, Europe and other Asia. With a facility area of more than 100,000 square meters，JHHY is able to manufacture over half million square meters per year and more than 6,000 items per month, whose PCB products range from 2 to 12 layers, HDI, Heavy Copper, and Embedded Capacitance, and provides "PCB One-stop Shop" service to meet customers’ diverse demands. The mission of JHHY is “Focused on technology innovation and customer's full satisfaction”. The core value of JHHY is “Customer-centric and Market-oriented”. With good mission and core value, JHHY will fulfill its great vision of “To be the top PCB enterprise and create maximum value for our clients, employees, society and shareholders”.
