1. Send inquiry to JHHY

You can send inquiry by E-mail or any other contact method, please including Gerber file, and also let us know your requirements about: quantity, lead-time, material, thickness, solder mask color, silk screen color, surface finish, finished copper thickness.

2. JHHY quotes

We will quote based on your files immediately.

3. Customer confirm the production file and price

We will handle the Gerble files and send it back for your confirm, although this step cost some time, but it will make sure the PCBs that we produce are just what you need, and it avoid quality problems. Once we get your confirm about the file and price, PI will be sent to you for payment.

4. Payment arrange

Customer arrange payment according to the PI

5. JHHY start fabrication

JHHY start production according to the files that customer confirmed.

6. Delivery

JHHY delivery once PCBs were tested and packed, tracking number will be sent to customer immediately after delivered.

7. Customer receive PCBs

Please kindly let us know once you received your PCBs.
